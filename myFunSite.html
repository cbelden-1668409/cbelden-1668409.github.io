Welcome to Experince Music

See a festival you want to go to, but don't know where to start? We've got you covered.

Here at Experience Music, we love going to a great music festival with our friends. We wanted to bring the joy we feel to you, for much less hassle and a reasonable price.

When you chose to work with us, you get excellent one-on-one care to make sure you have the best possible time. We book flights, hotels, camp grounds, rental cars and even occasionally buy your ticket right when they come out. We have made it easy to organize your group to go and we will try our best to make sure you guys are booked on the same flight and staying at the same hotel.
